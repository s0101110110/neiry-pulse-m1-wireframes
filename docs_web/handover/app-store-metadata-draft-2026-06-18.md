— ТРЕНИРОВКИ В ВАШИХ ЗОНАХ —

Ночью браслет фиксирует HRV, пульс покоя и фазы сна. Утренний отчёт показывает, насколько ночь помогла восстановиться.

## EN · Long description

```
Pulse Neiry is the companion app for your Neiry wristband. Learn your personal heart rate and HRV baseline — and finally understand what your body is telling you.

— YOUR BASELINE, NOT THE TEXTBOOK AVERAGE —
In the first 36–48 hours, Pulse calibrates your personal HRV (heart rate variability) and resting heart rate baseline. From there, you see deviations from your own norm — not generic ranges from a wellness blog.

— RECOVERY & READINESS —
Every morning Pulse tells you if your body is ready for load. HRV insights suggest when to ease off and when to push harder.

— TRAIN IN YOUR ZONES —
Start a workout right from the app. Pulse shows heart-rate zones, distance, GPS route, and calories in real time. After the session — a full breakdown anchored to your baseline.

— SLEEP & RECOVERY —
Overnight, your wristband records HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep stages. The morning report shows how well the night actually recovered you.

— CARE FOR YOUR FAMILY —
Share your readings with family members through a secure link. Useful for parents or relatives who care about your health.

— YOUR DATA, YOURS —
Pulse Neiry complies with Russian Federal Law 152-FZ on personal data. Data is encrypted; you control who sees what.

— REQUIRED —
Neiry wristband (VITRO / VANTA / VIGOR models). Bluetooth.

Pulse Neiry is for people who want to understand their body better than «10,000 steps a day».
```

**Chars:** ~1660

## Privacy / Compliance

- **Privacy policy URL:** TBD от Алисы (отдельный домен, vendor подтверждается 19.06). Apple template adapted + 152-ФЗ.
- **Data collected (App Privacy section iOS):**
  - Health data: HRV, heart rate, sleep, activity — used for app functionality (личная норма) и analytics (расход калорий)
  - User identifiers: email — used for account
  - Diagnostics: crash reports — Sentry / Firebase Crashlytics
  - **Не собираем:** location share с третьими сторонами, рекламные идентификаторы, тренировки без согласия пользователя
- **Data deletion:** Settings → Приватность → «Удалить аккаунт и все данные» (в скоупе MVP по PRD v2.6 §3.6)
